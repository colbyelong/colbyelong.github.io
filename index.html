Ph.D., Mathematics

In my research, I use tools from algebraic geometry, algebraic statistics, and discrete mathematics to address questions in phylogenetics. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at The College of Wooster. Before coming to Wooster, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Mathematical Biosciences Institute at The Ohio State University.
